Biography

Mohammad Hasyim Taufik is a student of Earth Science and Engineering Faculty, at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology. His current research interests include quantitative seismic interpretation, seismic imaging, and applied machine learning. He is currently interested in exploring and deploying machine learning applications on seismic data.

Prior to this, He has been involved in various laboratory assistantships during his bachelor degree and a professional experience as an exploration geophysicist which focused on the giant Kutei Basin, Eastern Kalimantan, Indonesia.
